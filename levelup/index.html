Laptop Donation Processing

Webcam

  1. Click "Start Camera" and accept the site's request to use your camera.
  2. If the camera is functional, a live feed should appear below. In this case, click "Webcam Functional."
  3. If the live feed does not appear within 10 sec, the camera is likely not functional. In this case, click, "Webcam Non-Functional"



Audio

Run the test twice: once without headphones connected and once with headphones connected.

  1. Click "Start Recording" and accept the site's request to use the microphone.
  2. Wait 2 sec for recording to start, then speak for a few seconds.
  3. Click "Stop Recording", the click "Play Recording"



Keyboard

Click the button to open a keyboard tester in a new page. Verify that all keys are functional.
